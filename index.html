Web design

When learning about a new company or a small business, most people today choose the internet as their primary source of information. That’s why it is so important to have a web presence that represents you, whether you want to show them your ideas or what’s on the menu.

How does it work?

Contact

The first step is to contact me and tell me what business you’re building. You can share your requests and plans or leave it up to me.

First sketch

After you’ve shown me the ideas you have about the project, I’ll be working on a design that I think suits your needs the best.

Final design

Once you agree upon a sketch you like, I will begin to code the actual webpage, which will later represent your brand.

Set up

Until now, the webpage was nothing but a file on a computer. To change this, someone needs to register the domain, arrange the hosting, and finally upload the site to the internet. This can all be hard and overwhelming for a beginner, so I will arrange all this for you.

Finished

Congratulations! You did it! Your website is now up and running and it can be accessed by anyone from around the world.

FAQ

How long does it take for you to finish all of this?

This of course depends on a lot of variables; how quickly a client reacts to messages, and how big the project is. But if you can reply within a reasonable time and just want a basic website, it shouldn’t take much longer than two weeks.

How much does it cost?

Again, this is based on several factors including the size of the project and the demands you have, but if you just want a simple static website, you can expect a price of around 120€. But you also have to pay some additional fees to the hosting provider for things like domain registration and the hosting itself - this can cost anything from 20€ to approximately 40€ depending on what provider you choose.

— What if I want to edit the website after it is already running? —

Whether you want to change a menu item or the opening hours, you have many options to edit the site. Either you ask a web developer to change something for you, or I can implement a system, that updates certain parts of the website based on a google spreadsheet, so you can change it fast and without any hassle.
